## 📝 CONTENT WRITING GUIDELINES

### Headline Formula
- **Benefit-focused**: Start with the primary benefit
- **Numbers included**: Quantify results when possible
- **Action-oriented**: Use strong verbs
- **Length**: 50-60 characters for mobile optimization

### Subheadline Formula
- **Problem-solution**: Acknowledge pain point, present solution
- **Length**: 120-150 characters
- **Specificity**: Include exact metrics/results

### Social Proof Structure
- **Name, Title, Company**
- **Specific metric achieved**
- **Brief testimonial quote**
- **Photo/video if possible**

### FAQ Writing
- **Question format**: Start with "How", "What", "Why"
- **Answer length**: 2-3 sentences
- **Value-focused**: Include benefits, not just features
