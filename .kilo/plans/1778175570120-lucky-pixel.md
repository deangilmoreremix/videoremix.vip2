# Plan: Refocus Landing Page from Video Creation to Personalized AI Marketing

## Objective
Transform the entire landing page content to emphasize personalized AI marketing instead of video creation, while preserving the existing structure and content volume. The focus should shift from "creating videos" to "personalized marketing campaigns using AI."

## Current Analysis
After reviewing all landing page components, extensive references to "video" exist throughout:
- **SpecialHero**: Platform badge, headline, subtext, CTA, and dashboard mockup
- **ProblemSection**: Problem descriptions and stats reference videos
- **SolutionSection**: Platform description, comparison points, and mockup interface
- **BenefitsSection**: Benefit descriptions, stats, and testimonials mention videos
- **ToolsCarouselSection**: Tool names and descriptions (e.g., "Video Generator")
- **PersonalizationWorkflowSection**: Workflow descriptions mention marketing videos
- **PricingSection**: Plan features reference video exports and quality
- **DemoSection**: References to video creation
- Other components have scattered video references

## Proposed Changes

- Change badge from "AI-Powered Video Platform" to "AI-Powered Marketing Platform"
- Update headline from "Create Videos That Convert Like Magic ✨" to "Personalized Marketing That Converts Like Magic ✨"
- Modify subtext from "personalized, high-converting videos in minutes — not hours" to "personalized, high-converting marketing campaigns in minutes — not hours"
- Update CTA button text from "Start Creating Free" to "Start Marketing Free"
- Modify mockup dashboard features from "AI Video Editing" to "AI Marketing Tools"
- Update mockup stats from videos to marketing metrics
- Change achievement notification from "First video created" to "First campaign created"
