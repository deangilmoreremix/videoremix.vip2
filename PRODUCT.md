# PRODUCT.md — VideoRemix.vip

## Product Purpose

VideoRemix.vip is an AI-powered video personalization platform that transforms marketing videos at scale. The platform enables businesses to create personalized video experiences for their customers using AI agents.

## Users

**Primary:**
- Marketing teams at mid-size to enterprise companies
- Digital agencies managing client video campaigns
- E-commerce brands with large product catalogs

**Context:** These users work in fast-paced environments, often under tight deadlines. They need powerful tools that are intuitive enough for non-technical team members to use.

**Where:** Desktop-primary (dashboard work), some mobile viewing of final outputs. Office environment with dual monitors common.

## Brand Voice

**Three words:** Technical, Empowering, Premium

- **Technical** — We know our stuff. Precise terminology, no fluff, confident about AI/automation capabilities.
- **Empowering** — Users feel capable. "You can do this" not "we do this for you."
- **Premium** — High-end service feel. Not corporate-sterile, not startup-gimmicky. Think: Stripe meets Webflow.

## Anti-References

- **Not:** Cute mascots, bubble letters, gradient-heavy "modern startup" aesthetics
- **Not:** Overly complex enterprise dashboards that intimidate
- **Not:** Stock photo libraries with fake smiling people

## Strategic Principles

1. **Show, don't tell** — Live demos beat feature lists
2. **Transparency builds trust** — Show the AI working, not a black box
3. **Scale is the product** — Every feature emphasizes "at scale"
4. **Personalization is the differentiator** — Generic video is the problem we solve

## Register

**Brand** — The landing page IS the product showcase. Visitors should immediately grasp the power of AI video personalization.
