# PRODUCT.md — VideoRemix.vip

## Product Purpose

VideoRemix.vip is an AI-powered marketing personalization platform that transforms generic marketing content into personalized experiences at scale. The platform enables businesses to create hyper-personalized marketing campaigns using AI agents that analyze customer data and dynamically customize content.

## Problem Statement

Traditional marketing is generic and impersonal. Businesses spend significant resources creating campaigns that speak to everyone but resonate with no one. VideoRemix.vip solves this by enabling hyper-personalized marketing content at enterprise scale.

## Solution Overview

VideoRemix.vip uses advanced AI agents to automatically personalize marketing content based on customer data, preferences, and behavior. Users upload base content and customer datasets, then our AI generates thousands of personalized variants instantly.
