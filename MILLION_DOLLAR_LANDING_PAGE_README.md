### Revolutionary AI-Powered Targeting

The **AI Creative Personalizer** is our flagship feature that transforms generic marketing into hyper-personalized campaigns through:

#### 1. **Public Profile Scanning (Maigret Integration)**
- **Multi-Username Support**: Scan multiple social profiles simultaneously
- **Site Tag Filtering**: Include/exclude platforms by category (gaming, coding, social, business)
- **Advanced Options**:
  - Top sites limit (default 500, up to 10,000)
  - Username permutations (generate variations)
  - Recursive search (find linked profiles)
  - Information extraction (parse profile data)

1. **Cold Email**: Personalized outreach with offer/goal/tone/CTA

6. **Content Campaign**: Multi-piece content strategy
7. **Agency Pitch**: Agency presentation decks
8. **Lead Summary**: Comprehensive lead profiles with insights

- **App-Specific Prompts**: Different templates for each of the 116+ apps
